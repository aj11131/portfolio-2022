About me

Overview

Below I have listed three traits I believe make me an effective software engineer:

  1. Problem Solver - I am an expert problem solver.
  2. Adaptable - I am extremely adaptable and able to quickly pick up new technologies and processes
  3. Generalist - I like to understand how all parts of a system operate (i.e. data storage, APIs, and client). I believe having at least an understanding of how all these components function allow you produce higher quality code.
